Project One

What Areas are most at Risk of Posionous Tap Water in Flint, Michigan?

The idea for this project was to identify increased risk areas in Flint, Michigan by identifying certain harmful features. The features that we focused on were lead piping, facilities with over 10,000 tons of water pollution, and rivers. Important tools for this project include project, join, buffer, clip, dissolve attributes, and union.

Project Two

What Areas are most at Risk of Posionous Tap Water in Flint, Michigan?

How can we select areas that may be suitable for the opening of a new healthy food store?

The features that we focused on were public transportation, healthy food stores, and empowerment zones. Important tools that were used for this project include union, intersect, erase, clip, overlay, dissolve, and buffer. An interesting problem in this project was about excluding areas that already had healthy food stores near by. The erase tool was helpful to remove areas already with access to healthy food stores from the target area.
